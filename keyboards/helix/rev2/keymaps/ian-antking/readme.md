# The a qwerty layout for typists who switch keyboard a lot.
## Layout

### Qwerty
```
    ,-----------------------------------------.             ,-----------------------------------------.
    | Esc  |   1  |   2  |   3  |   4  |   5  |             |   6  |   7  |   8  |   9  |   0  | Del  |
    |------+------+------+------+------+------|             |------+------+------+------+------+------|
    |  `   |   Q  |   W  |   E  |   R  |   T  |             |   Y  |   U  |   I  |   O  |   P  | Bksp |
    |------+------+------+------+------+------|             |------+------+------+------+------+------|
    | Tab  |   A  |   S  |   D  |   F  |   G  |             |   H  |   J  |   K  |   L  |   ;  |  '   |
    |------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------|
    | Shift|   Z  |   X  |   C  |   V  |   B  | `    | '    |   N  |   M  |   ,  |   .  |   /  |Ent/sf|
    |------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------|
    |Adjust| Ctl  | Alt  | Alt  | GUI  |Lower |Space |Space |Raise | Gui  | Left | Down |  Up  | Right|
    `-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------'
```

## Layers

|Priority|number|name|description|
| ---- | ---- | --- | --- |
|high|16|Adjust|Functions|
||2|Raise|Numeric charactors|
||1|Lower|Other charactors|
|low|0|Qwerty|QWERTY leyout(base)|
